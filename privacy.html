Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

Thank you for using Focus Tab — a Chrome extension that helps you focus by hiding all tabs except the one you are currently viewing, and easily restoring them later.

Data Collection

Focus Tab does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). The extension operates entirely within your local browser environment.

Permissions

The extension requests certain Chrome permissions only for functionality:

  • tabs: To hide, restore, and manage your open tabs.
  • windows: To focus on a single window and restore others.
  • storage: To locally store which tabs were hidden, if needed.
  • activeTab: To identify the tab currently in focus.

These permissions are used strictly for providing the intended functionality — no data is shared externally.

Third-Party Services

Focus Tab does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or connect to any external servers or APIs.
